title: "Fundamentals"
date: 2026-03-30
---
Learning the fundamentals feels like a big step, because in many ways it is—the first steps 
are the ones that shape everything that comes after. 
It can be slow and sometimes frustrating, 
but each concept builds a base that makes future problems easier to understand and solve. 
What seems simple on the surface often carries the most weight, 
and getting these basics right makes a real difference later on.
